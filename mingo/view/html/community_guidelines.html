COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

Postify has a set of Community Guidelines outlining what type of content is and isn't permitted on our platform. We implement our Community Guidelines and apply them equally to everyone to fulfill our objective of creating a secure community while also allowing content creators to share their inspiration and perspectives.

The areas covered by our Community Guidelines are as follows.

  • IMPERSONATION, as the safety of our creators, viewers, and partners is our first priority. We are counting on each of you to assist us in protecting this dynamic community. We do not accept a profile that mimics another profile's content and background, as this will confuse everyone.

  • CHILD SAFETY, we do not accept content that is aimed at young children and families but contains sexual themes, violence, obscene content, or other mature topics that are inappropriate for young audiences and jeopardize minors' emotional and physical well-being.

  • PORNOGRAPHY, explicit content such as showing sexual actions, fetishes, or showing genitals are not permitted on our platform since they may affect the thoughts and conduct of our young audiences.

  • SELF-HARM: Because the health of our content producers and viewers is essential to us, we do not allow content that promotes self-harm and puts viewers at danger. We only encourage people to share how they cope with or overcome mental health issues.
