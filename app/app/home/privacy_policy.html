PRIVACY POLICY

We collect information about the apps, browsers, and devices you use to access Google services, which allows us to deliver features such as ensuring that you have the best experience possible if you are using an Android device.

We use the information we gather, such as your email address, to connect with you. To send you news about planned changes or upgrades to our platform.
